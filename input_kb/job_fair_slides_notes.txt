Job Fair Preparation Notes
==========================

- Always research the company before approaching their stall.
- Carry at least 5 printed copies of your tailored resume.
- Prepare a 30-second self introduction (elevator pitch).
- Be ready to explain your top project in 2 minutes.
- Ask thoughtful questions about the role and team.
- Collect business cards and follow up via email within 48 hours.

Common Recruiter Questions at Job Fairs
- Tell me about yourself.
- Why are you interested in our company?
- Walk me through your best project.
- What technologies are you most comfortable with?
- Are you open to internships, part-time, or full-time?

Dressing and Etiquette
- Dress smart-casual or business formal.
- Maintain eye contact and a confident handshake.
- Keep your phone on silent during conversations.

Interview Preparation Notes
===========================

Technical Preparation
- Revise Python basics: data types, loops, functions, OOP.
- Understand the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning.
- Be able to explain overfitting, underfitting, and how to avoid them.
- Know how to clean data using Pandas (missing values, duplicates).
- Understand basic SQL: SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY, WHERE.
- Be ready to whiteboard a small problem (e.g., reverse a string).

Project Discussion Tips
- Use the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result.
- Quantify impact whenever possible (e.g., improved accuracy by 8%).
- Be honest about what you did vs. what your teammates did.
- Mention what you learned and what you would improve.

Behavioral and HR Questions
- Tell me about yourself.
- Why should we hire you?
- What are your strengths and weaknesses?
- Describe a challenge you faced and how you handled it.
- Where do you see yourself in 3 years?

Closing the Interview
- Always prepare 2-3 questions to ask the interviewer.
- Thank the interviewer and ask about next steps.
- Send a short thank-you email within 24 hours.
